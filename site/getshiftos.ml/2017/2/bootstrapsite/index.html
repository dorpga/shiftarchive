What is ShiftOS?

An operating system

ShiftOS is the most advanced, customizable operating system in the world. Using utilities like the ShiftFS filesystem and the Shifter Skin System, we've spent hours of coding in a bedroom on an over-powered dev environment to make ShiftOS user-best-friendly.

A community

When you turn on your computer with ShiftOS installed, you are instantly connected to a network of other ShiftOS users called the multi-user domain. You can share programs, files, and even talk with other users right inside ShiftOS. We also have a forum and a Discord server.

Years of pride, work and dedication.

ShiftOS, lead by 15-year old C# programmer and high-school student Michael VanOverbeek, isn't an easy project. It took multiple development teams spanning years of development to get right. ShiftOS has quite the history - and through all its hardships, is still going strong.

Join the community

ShiftOS has an amazing community of developers, staff, and users. If you have a problem or just want to get in touch with us, we can be reached at our forum, or our Discord server.

Additionally, ShiftOS connects you to a multi-user domain with everyone else. If you want to hang out while in-game, use a multi-user domain chat if one's open!

Issues?

If you have a problem with the game, consider heading to our GitHub page! You can access it at any time using the 'See the code' button in the top-right corner of your screen. Then click Issues and have a look there!
